My Favorite Authors & Their Works

In no particular order...

  • Margaret Atwood
  • Lauren Slater
  • Kathryn Harrison
  • Caroline Knapp

Margaret Atwood

Known for her dystopian and science fiction settings, Margaret Atwood presents a unique societal and gender commentary.

Some of my favorite works of hers:

  1. The Edible Woman
  2. Cat's Eye
  3. The Robber Bride

Lauren Slater

From her experience with epilepsy and an unique childhood, Lauren weaves etheral tales of psychiatric care in America.

Some of my favorite works of hers:

  1. Lying: A Metaphorical Memoir
  2. Welcome to My Country
  3. Playing House: Notes of a Reluctant Mother

Kathryn Harrison

Kathryn takes the reader to uncomfortable places in her no-holds bared account of her family.

Some of my favorite works of hers:

  1. The Kiss
  2. The Mother Knot
  3. The Road to Santiago

Caroline Knapp

Caroline candidly tells of her battle with alchohol, aneroxia and the profound love she feels for her dog and her friends.

Some of my favorite works of hers:

  1. Drinking: A Love Story
  2. Pack of Two: The Incritate Bond Between People and Dogs
  3. Appetites
